E) How Get Google Key for Instant Indexing - top

  1. Login to Google Developer Console using your Google account credentials.
  2. If you are not registered on Google developer account, then you need to accept agreement.
  • After accepting agreement or if you are already registered on Google developer account, you will see a screen as shown below. Click on “Select a project” on top header bar.
  • From the project selection popup click on the "NEW PROJECT" button to add a new project.
  • Enter your “Project name” and click on the “Create” button.
  • Navigate to APIs & Service section and click for Library.
  • Search for Indexing in Search form, and select Web Search Indexing API.
  • Enable it.
  • Move to dashboard, under APIs & Services.
  • Click Credentials in menu bar, and click the CREAT CREDENTIALS button.
  • Click Service Account .
  • Enter service name .
  • Service description (optional).
  • Press Create and continue button
  • Select the role as owner.
  • click continue and done.
  • Now You successfully create you service account, you would see your service Email, copy it and save it some where you would need it later .
  • Click the Email link .
  • Navigate to KEY section.
  • Click Add key, and choose Create new Key .
  • Choose JSON, and click Create.
  • it would download it in your device.
  • It would something like this.
  • now go to your Google Search Console.
  • navigate to your current site / project.
  • Go to Settings .
  • Click on Users and permission.
  • Click on Add user button.
  • Paste that your Google Service Email Address you copy
  • and set the permission as OWNER
  • Go to your Site admin panel in setting and activate the Instant Indexing plugin.
  • Go to Instant Indexing Page from Menu.
  • Upload the Google Service Key .json file to the Setting session.

F) How Get PAYSTACK API Public and Secret KEY - top

Follow the below steps to get Paystack API Keys:

  1. Login to your Paystack account and navigate to Dasboard
  2. If you did not have account you have to create it here Register
  3. Set your account details andd all the requirements .
  4. Create / Add new Business and submit for paystack verification, if your are verified then proceed
  5. At the top from right Toogle your account as live, under your project.
  6. Mavigate to Setting from menu.
  7. Mavigate to API Keys & Webhooks.
  8. Copy your live Public Key .
  9. Generate New live Secret Key, and copy.
